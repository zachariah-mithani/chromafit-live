Managing Your Subscription:

To view, change, or cancel your ChromaFit Pro subscription:

  • Open Settings on your iPhone
  • Tap your name at the top
  • Tap Subscriptions
  • Select ChromaFit
  • Choose your preferred option (cancel, change plan, etc.)

Restoring Purchases:

If you previously purchased ChromaFit Pro and it's not showing:

  • Open ChromaFit
  • Go to Settings
  • Tap "Restore Purchases"

Refund Requests:

For refunds, please contact Apple directly at reportaproblem.apple.com

üèÉ Health Data & Syncing

Steps, Calories, or Weight Not Syncing?

ChromaFit integrates with Apple HealthKit. To ensure proper syncing:

  • Open Settings > Health
  • Tap Data Access & Devices
  • Select ChromaFit
  • Make sure all relevant categories are turned ON (Steps, Active Energy, Dietary Energy, Body Mass)

Water Tracking:

Water intake is tracked directly in ChromaFit (not HealthKit). Simply tap the quick-add buttons (+8, +16, or custom) to log your hydration throughout the day.

üí™ Physique Lab & AI Analysis

Getting Accurate Physique Scores:

For best results when scanning your physique:

  • Use good lighting (natural light works best)
  • Stand against a plain background
  • Take photos from front, back, or side angles
  • Wear form-fitting clothing or take shirtless photos
  • Stand with arms slightly away from body

Workout Plan Generation:

After receiving your AI scores, you can generate personalized 3-6 day workout splits. Pro members get 20 workout plans per month, while free users get 3.

Scan Limits:

  • Free: 3 physique scans/month
  • Pro: 50 physique scans/month

üçî AI Food Scanner

Getting Accurate Meal Analysis:

For best scanning results:

  • Take photos in good lighting
  • Capture the entire meal in frame
  • Avoid blurry or dark photos
  • Include all components of your meal

Food Scanner Limits:

  • Free: 1 food scan/month
  • Pro: Unlimited food scanning with full macro breakdown

Note: AI estimates are approximate. For critical dietary needs, consult nutrition labels or a registered dietitian.
